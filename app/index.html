About Me

Myself

Hello! I’m Saajid Khan, a Software Developer with over three years of development experience.

I’ve always been a great problem solver, an independent learner, and a technophile obsessed with the latest devices. Today, I’m working as a Software Developer for FCB Health, where I get to show off all these elements of who I am.

I started learning to code during college though it was always more of a hobby than a career focus. After a college education in Biology, and continuing to pursue that hobby, I realized software engineering was the right field for me.

Since then, I’ve worked on countless projects and have been involved with a handful of notable companies within the healthcare and pharmaceutical space.
